Everybody Hates Chris

This is an American semi-autobiographical sitcom created by Chris Rock and Ali LeRoi

The show is inspired by the memories of Rock as the teenager. The show is set from 1982 to 1987, but Rock himself was actually a teenager from 1978 to 1985, having been born in 1965

Studio

Paramount Television Studios was part of the original developers together with Fox then picked up by UPN which later merged with The WB to form The CW. The show ended in 2009.
